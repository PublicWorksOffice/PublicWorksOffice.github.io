Public Works Office
About

Public Works Office (PwO) exists to serve those who serve the public.

PwO provides strategic advice related to technology and digital customer experience, especially as the two intersect with the built urban environment.

Via a combination of strategic advice and delivery, PwO will work with you to understand your organizational goals, translate those to a set of technical goals, outline a roadmap to get there, and execute on the agreed upon plan.

PwO's founder Jeff Maki has 15+ years of experience delivering user-centric technical products and solutions.

  • Product Development, including roadmapping, definition and measurement of KPIs and product feature definition
  • Agile Development Coaching, including Scrum and delivery processes
  • Interim CTO/CIO
Delivery
  • Software Development, both front- and back-end
  • DevOps, including cloud migrations
Business Development
  • RFP Responses
  • RFP Development
